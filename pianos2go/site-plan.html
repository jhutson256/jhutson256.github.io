Overview

Purpose

This website was made by a professional piano mover for people who want to know the best ways to move a piano. Whether you're planning on moving a piano yourself or you're just curious, this advice can have practical use for you. This website is full of advice on the safest, easiest ways to move any piano. From the best equipment to use, to the best ways to manipulate the weight of a piano making it easier to move, and even the proper ways to safely transport pianos. With this advice, you'll be a piano moving pro just like me, and better yet, you won't have to visit the chiropractor afterwards.

Audience

The intended audience for this website is anyone who has the size and strength to move a piano. That age range would typically fall between 16 to about 60 to 70 years old. The biggest deciding factor on who can move a piano is the person's overall strength. If you can lift around 500 pounds, then you can move a piano. This website will best serve those who want or need to move a piano, but don't know the best ways to move a piano without hurting themselves.
